History

James Whittemore is a co-founder of Executive Thrillseeker travel blog, a full-time travel writer and content creator. When he is not traveling, he is based out of New York with his wife and travel partner Inna. Together they visit new places to find the best White Water Rafting locations. After doing this for 3 years, James became an expert in rafting destinations, especially in Southeast Asia and the United States.
